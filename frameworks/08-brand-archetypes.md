# Framework: Brand Archetypes
> Source: Carl Jung / Carol Pearson, The Hero and the Outlaw
> Use for: voice development, visual identity, community feel, content tone

---

## The 12 Archetypes

| Archetype | Core desire | Fear | Tone |
|-----------|------------|------|------|
| **The Sage** | Truth and wisdom | Ignorance, being misled | Authoritative, thoughtful, educational |
| **The Creator** | Innovation, self-expression | Mediocrity, being inauthentic | Imaginative, provocative, original |
| **The Hero** | Mastery, achievement | Weakness, failure | Courageous, bold, inspiring |
| **The Caregiver** | Service, protection | Selfishness, ingratitude | Warm, nurturing, supportive |
| **The Rebel** | Revolution, disruption | Conformity, powerlessness | Bold, provocative, challenging norms |
| **The Explorer** | Freedom, discovery | Constraint, boredom | Adventurous, independent, pioneering |
| **The Innocent** | Safety, goodness | Corruption, wrongdoing | Optimistic, simple, pure |
| **The Lover** | Intimacy, beauty | Disconnection, being unloved | Passionate, sensory, intimate |
| **The Jester** | Joy, play | Boring, serious | Funny, irreverent, light |
| **The Magician** | Transformation | Unintended consequences | Visionary, transformative, mystical |
| **The Ruler** | Control, order | Chaos, being overthrown | Authoritative, responsible, polished |
| **The Everyman** | Belonging, connection | Exclusion, standing out | Friendly, humble, relatable |

---

## How to Identify Your Archetype

Ask:
1. What does your brand fundamentally stand for?
2. What does your community feel when they're in the room with you?
3. What would your best client say you gave them beyond the practical result?
4. Which archetype do your best-ever clients naturally embody?

Brands work best with one primary archetype and one secondary. More than two creates incoherence.

---

## Common Mistakes

- **Sage + Rebel:** Conflicting. Authority and disruption pull in opposite directions.
- **Caregiver + Hero:** Works but the Hero energy can feel cold if overemphasized.
- **Everyman positioning at premium prices:** Breaks. Premium = Ruler, Sage, Creator, or Magician energy.
