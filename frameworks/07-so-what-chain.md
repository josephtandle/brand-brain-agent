# Framework: The So What Chain
> Source: Referenced in Age of AI Workshop Part 2
> Use for: translating features into felt outcomes, homepage copy, Mental Market positioning

---

## The Method

Take any feature, deliverable, or outcome. Ask "So what?" repeatedly until you reach an identity statement or a felt emotion. That is the Mental Market version.

The chain usually takes 3-5 "so whats" to reach the real answer.

---

## Examples

**Feature:** "Weekly live sessions"
So what? → You build something every week.
So what? → Your business actually changes instead of you just learning about change.
So what? → You stop being the person who knows what to do and start being the person whose business does it.
**Felt outcome:** "I am someone whose business moves forward without me having to manage every step."

---

**Feature:** "100+ hours saved per month"
So what? → You have more time.
So what? → You can spend it on the work only you can do.
So what? → You stop being the bottleneck and start being the reason the business works.
So what? → Your clients get more of you, not less.
**Felt outcome:** "I show up fully for the people I'm actually here to serve."

---

**Feature:** "Custom AI system built for your business"
So what? → It runs your operations.
So what? → You stop being the operator.
So what? → Your business runs while you sleep, travel, or focus on what matters.
So what? → You built something that doesn't need you to be there every day.
**Felt outcome:** "I built a business, not a job."

---

## How to Apply

1. List every feature or deliverable in your offer.
2. Run the So What Chain on each one.
3. Find the 2-3 chains that produce the most vivid felt outcomes.
4. Lead your homepage, ads, and content with those felt outcomes — not the features.

---

## Rule

If you can replace the word "so" with "so I can feel like" and the sentence still makes sense, you have found the Mental Market version.

"100 hours saved per month so I can feel like someone who runs a real business and not a hamster wheel." Yes. That is the real outcome.
